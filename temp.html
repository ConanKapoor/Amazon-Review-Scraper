5.0 out of 5 stars Cracker of a phone!
By Advocatus Diaboli on 14 December 2017
Size: 64 GB | Verified Purchase
Despite a delivery hiccup by Amazon which was efficiently sorted by their customer service, I can only give this phone and Amazon a full 5 star rating. It's a classic looking mobile that performs brilliantly. A great camera belies it's humble pricing. I'd ordered this phone for my Mother and was thinking of getting the Apple X for myself. This is a good phone for a couple of price bands higher than where it sits. The display is bright yet not harsh on the eyes.(which is why I don't like Samsung phones) Needless to add, I'll be waiting for 1+6 iteration. Apple X is out of the reckoning for me.
